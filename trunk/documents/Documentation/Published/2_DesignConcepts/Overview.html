Design Concepts

Bindable LINQ is a library of extensions to LINQ that enable reactive programming via LINQ queries. This section provides an overview of the design concepts that make up Bindable LINQ, and is split into two parts: a background to Bindable LINQ and the problems it helps you solve, and a detailed look at various parts that make up Bindable LINQ.
